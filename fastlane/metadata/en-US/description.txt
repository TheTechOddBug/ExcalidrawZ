ExcalidrawZ is a native Excalidraw client for Mac, iPad, and iPhone, designed for hand-drawn whiteboards, diagrams, sketches, visual notes, and technical explanations.

It keeps the familiar Excalidraw canvas experience, then adds native file management, iCloud sync, history, import and export tools, and Apple-platform interactions around it.

Hand-drawn whiteboards and diagrams
- Create flowcharts, architecture diagrams, wireframes, study notes, and visual sketches
- Work with the familiar hand-drawn Excalidraw style
- Use Apple Pencil, keyboard shortcuts, mouse, and trackpad controls
- Continue editing across Mac, iPad, and iPhone

Manage Excalidraw files
- Autosave drawings managed inside the app
- Organize work with groups, sorting, and local folders
- Sync app-managed files across devices with iCloud
- Review file history and restore important versions
- Open and edit .excalidraw, .excalidraw.png, and .excalidraw.svg files

Import, export, and share
- Import PDFs and organize them on the canvas
- Convert Mermaid diagrams into editable Excalidraw content
- Insert LaTeX formulas and function graphs
- Export images, PDFs, Excalidraw files, and editable PNG/SVG files
- Share through the clipboard, system share sheet, and backup archives

More advanced tools
- Use Excalidraw collaboration when drawing with others
- Ask AI to understand, create, and revise canvas content
- Preview AI-generated proposals before applying them to your real canvas
- Lock sensitive files and control which content is visible to AI
- Expose MCP services for compatible AI clients

ExcalidrawZ is useful for product design, engineering notes, study diagrams, teaching, documentation, brainstorming, and everyday whiteboard work.
